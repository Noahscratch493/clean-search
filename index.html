What "-ai -wikipedia" Does

Removes:

  • AI-generated blog posts and articles
  • Wikipedia entries (often too basic)
  • Auto-generated content farms
  • Low-quality AI summaries

Keeps:

  • Expert-written articles
  • Forum discussions and Q&A
  • Personal blogs and experiences
  • Original research and reports

Use This Trick Anywhere

Add this to any Google search to get cleaner results:

-ai -wikipedia

Just add this to the end of your search query
